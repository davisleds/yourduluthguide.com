HOW TO USE IN GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE:
------------------------------------
1. Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Select your property (duluthguide.com or duluthstay.com)
3. Use the URL Inspection tool at the top
4. Paste each URL and click "Request Indexing"
5. Wait for Google to process (can take a few minutes to days)

PRIORITY ORDER FOR INSPECTION:
-------------------------------
1. Homepage (index.html or /)
2. Restaurants page (main content page)
3. Favicon (GoogleIcon.png)
4. Dining page
5. Travel guide page
6. Sitemap.xml
7. Logo.svg (if needed)

NOTE: After requesting indexing for the favicon URL specifically, 
it may help speed up Google recognizing the new favicon.
